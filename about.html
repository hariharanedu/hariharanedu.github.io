Our Story

It all started with a simple frustration: scrolling through endless Telegram channels just to find a single movie link that works. The process was tedious, time-consuming, and often led to dead ends.

We decided to change that. We built Mrunalini Bot, a powerful indexing engine that organizes publicly available files and makes them searchable in milliseconds. What begun as a small personal project has now grown into a massive ecosystem serving thousands of users daily.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, fast, and completely free way for users to discover entertainment content on Telegram, without the hassle of intrusive ads or broken links.
